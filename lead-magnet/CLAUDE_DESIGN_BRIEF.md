## What this is

A free PDF lead magnet for **SNC Advertising** — a senior-led Meta
performance-marketing agency for ecommerce founders in Central / Western
Europe. Visitors download this PDF in exchange for their email; it
demonstrates expertise and naturally upsells the agency's audit service.

**Title:** "The Meta Self-Audit"
**Sub:** "12 checks. 15 minutes. The list we run before quoting an audit."
**Audience:** ecommerce founders spending €1k+/month on Meta ads, ~28-45 years
old, numerate, allergic to marketing fluff, runs their own brand.
**Goal of the PDF:** be the kind of document the reader *keeps* and refers
back to, and that makes them think "these people know their stuff."

## Brand system (must hold)

### Voice / tone
- Ascetic. Confident. **Numbers over adjectives.**
- Short sentences. Sentence fragments are fine.
- No marketing jargon. Banned words: synergy, passionate, innovative,
  cutting-edge, best-in-class, unlock (verb), leverage (verb), world-class,
  next-level, "ultimate guide."
- No exclamation marks. No emojis.
- Tone: a senior practitioner telling a peer how it actually works.

### Typography
- **Single typeface: IBM Plex Mono.** Weights 300, 400, 600. No other font.
- Letter-spacing: tight on body, generous on tracked labels (`0.18–0.25em` on
  all-caps eyebrow/section labels).
- Body 10.5–11pt. Headlines 16–36pt depending on hierarchy.

### Colour palette
```
Money Green   #1E4D3A   primary, brand, headings on light, full-bleed covers
Engine Bronze #BF9340   accent (used sparingly — item numbers, "bad" signals)
Deep Forest   #0F2A20   dark panels (rarely used in a PDF)
Sage Lift     #7FA898   muted accent (use rarely)
Off-White     #F5F3EF   default page background (warm)
Near-Black    #0C0C0C   body text on light
Graphite      #3C3C3C   secondary body text
White         #FFFFFF   text on green panels
```

Usage ratio target: **Green 25% · Off-White 60% · Near-Black 10% · Bronze 5%.**
Bronze is the accent — use only on item numbers (01, 02 …) and on "Bad:"
signals. Never on body text.

### Visual system
- Hard left-align everywhere. Generous whitespace.
- Thin hairline rules (`1px` / `0.25mm`) as dividers, never thick boxes.
- Square corners (0 radius). No drop shadows. No gradients.
- Numbered items with leading-zero (`01`, `02`, … `12`) — bronze, mono, tracked.
- Section labels: `§ 1 — TRACKING & ATTRIBUTION` style — all caps, tracked, green.
- No imagery, no stock photos, no icons. **Type-only document.** This is a
  brand bet — keep it.
- If you must add a visual element, a single thin hairline diagram (data flow,
  funnel) is acceptable. No clip-art. No infographics.

### Reference
The visual identity is documented in the brand book at
`outputs_design/snc-brand-book.html` and the logo system at
`outputs_design/snc-logo-book.html`. If conflicts arise, the website at
`https://sncads.com/` is the canonical
implementation — match its discipline.

## Format

- **A4 portrait.** 210mm × 297mm. Print-ready PDF.
- **5–6 pages total** (don't bloat). Suggested:
  1. Cover (green full-bleed)
  2. Opening + § 1 (Tracking, 2 items)
  3. § 2 (Structure, 3 items)
  4. § 3 (Creative, 3 items)
  5. § 4 (Audience, 2 items)
  6. § 5 (Numbers, 2 items) + Closing + CTA
- Generous margins: ~18mm top/bottom, 20mm sides.
- Page numbers in footer: tiny, `01 / 06`, tracked, graphite.
- Cover: green full-bleed, large mono headline, no imagery.
- Body pages: off-white background, near-black text, green section labels,
  bronze item numbers.

## What to produce

A standalone HTML file that:
1. Renders A4 print-ready (use `@page` and `mm` units).
2. Prints to a clean 5–6 page PDF via Chrome's "Save as PDF" with no headers,
   no footers, margins set to None.
3. Loads IBM Plex Mono from Google Fonts (`?family=IBM+Plex+Mono:wght@300;400;600`).
4. Uses only the colour palette above.
5. Looks distinctively SNC — not a generic agency PDF.

---

## What to push further (vs the baseline HTML)

The baseline `meta-self-audit-en.html` covers the structure correctly but
the design can be more refined. Push these:
- **Cover composition** — the baseline cover is functional but could be
  bolder. The hero typography can be larger and more compositionally daring.
  Consider negative space, a thin geometric mark, a vertical rule.
- **Section openers** — make the §-number / section-title pairing more
  intentional. They currently live as a hairline rule + label. Could be a
  larger numeric statement.
- **Item rhythm** — the items are dense. Could benefit from more breathing
  room between items, especially the `Good/Bad` signal line.
- **Page-number footer** — currently absent. Add a tiny `01 / 06` style
  footer.
- **Cover-back / colophon** — the closing page could feel more like a
  signature than a footer.

Do not add:
- Imagery, photography, icons, gradients, drop shadows, rounded corners,
  serif type, sans-serif type.
- Anything that says "marketing agency PDF template."
