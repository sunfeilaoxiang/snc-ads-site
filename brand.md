## Positioning (one line)

> Senior-led performance marketing for ecommerce founders who want results, not agency theatre.

Internal reference: "the Porsche Boxster of performance agencies." **Never used customer-facing.**
The Boxster metaphor is for internal alignment only. Buyers don't care about it.

## Who we work with

- **Vertical:** Ecommerce founders (primary). Some lead-gen for service businesses (secondary).
- **Size:** Annual revenue **hard floor €300k/yr; target €500k+/yr.** Past the DIY-ads phase, not enterprise.
- **Geography:** Central & Western Europe.
- **Language:** Russian-speaking founders in diaspora (DE, CZ, PL, Baltics, CY, IL). English-speaking later.
- **Ad budget:** Hard floor **€1k/month** — a floor, not a target. Healthy sweet spot **€3k–€10k/month**, where the fee is a sane fraction of total marketing cost. Target the sweet spot; accept the floor only selectively.
- **Mindset:** Numerate. Comfortable in dashboards. Wants a partner, not a babysitter.

## What we sell

- **Meta paid media is the only Phase 1 service.** Two service lines: lead-gen (lower complexity) and ecommerce (higher complexity → higher price).
- Creative: brief + direction included; production outsourced and **invoiced to the client separately**, never bundled into the retainer.
- **TikTok — not in Phase 1.** Do not sell or advertise it on the site until the Meta system is mastered.
- Google Ads / SEO / Amazon DSP — out of scope. Later cross-sell only.
- Not influencer, not full-funnel "strategy." Stay narrow — one channel, known deeply.

## How we work (operational reality, must reconcile with brand voice)

- **Written-first, not text-only.** One monthly strategic call. Everything else in writing.
- **Briefs via template.** We give the client a structured intake form. They fill it. We execute.
- **Async cadence, not "same-day response."** Brand voice should say "deliberate cadence" / "no firefighting," not "instant replies."
- **Transparent reporting.** Live dashboard. Weekly written update. Monthly review doc.
- **Two founders + freelance creative.** Junior specialists added when client load demands.

## Brand voice — rules

- **Short sentences.** Most under 12 words. Sentence fragments are fine.
- **Numbers over adjectives.** "€340k ad spend, 4.2× ROAS, 90 days" beats "incredible results."
- **No marketing jargon.** Specifically banned: "synergy," "passionate," "innovative," "cutting-edge,"
  "best-in-class," "unlock," "leverage" (verb form), "world-class," "next-level."
- **Allowed but with care:** "performance," "ROAS," "creative testing," "compounding," "operating system."
- **No exclamation marks. No emojis. No question-headlines.** Statements only.
- **Founder voice is direct.** "We charge by complexity. We don't guarantee ROAS. We can usually tell in week 4 if we're a fit."

## Brand voice — Russian rules

- **Тон:** аскетичный, точный, без пафоса. Короткие предложения. Цифры, не прилагательные.
- **Запрещено:** «команда профессионалов», «индивидуальный подход», «лучшие специалисты», «комплексные решения», «мы поможем вашему бизнесу выйти на новый уровень».
- **Обращение к клиенту:** на «вы» (с маленькой буквы), без панибратства.
- **Анг. термины (ROAS, CPA, CPM, креатив, перформанс) — оставляем как есть**, не переводим. Целевая аудитория их знает.
- **Тире длинное (—), не дефис.** Кавычки «ёлочки», не "лапки".

## Visual system (must hold on Tilda pages)

- **Typeface:** IBM Plex Mono only. Weights 300, 400, 600. Load via Google Fonts in site head.
- **Colors:** Money Green `#1E4D3A` primary. Engine Bronze `#BF9340` accent (max 10% of any page). Deep Forest `#0F2A20` for dark sections. Off-White `#F5F3EF` for warm light sections. Near-Black `#0C0C0C` for dark canvas. White `#FFFFFF` for light canvas.
- **Usage ratio:** Green 65% / White or Off-White 25% / Bronze 10%.
- **Layout:** Generous whitespace. Hard left-align. Thin rule lines (`1px solid #1A1A1A` on dark; `1px solid #E5E5E5` on light) as primary divider.
- **Imagery:** Avoided. Brand is type-driven. When images required, use B&W or duotone (green + bronze) only. No stock photos of "diverse teams shaking hands."
- **Buttons:** Solid green on white, solid white on green, outlined bronze on off-white. No rounded corners (0 border-radius) or 2px max.

## CTAs

Primary: **"Получить аудит"** / "Get audit" — links to contact/inquiry form. The audit is a **qualify-gated hook**, not an instant deliverable: the inquiry is screened first, and the audit is done only for leads that fit. Site copy must not promise an instant/ungated free audit.
Secondary: **"Написать в Telegram"** / "Message on Telegram" — link to a Telegram chat.
Tertiary: **"Посмотреть кейсы"** / "See case studies" — links to case studies page.

## Things we never promise

- Guaranteed ROAS
- Guaranteed timeline ("results in 14 days")
- Specific number of leads/sales without seeing the account
- 24/7 support
- Same-day responses
- Free trials
- "Money-back guarantee"

## Things we do promise (and can deliver)

- Written weekly update by Friday EOD client time
- Live dashboard access from day 1
- Monthly review call (optional, 30 min)
- Account audit and benchmarks within 7 days of kickoff
- 30-day notice cancellation, no questions
